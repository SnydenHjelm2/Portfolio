About me

My main intrests are: NFL, Counter-Strike, Hiking, Food and of course Web-development! I got introduced to web-development during my first year of gymnasium and I fell for it instantly. I found something that I was passionate about, and wanted to take it further. Therefore I started my university studies in September of 2024.

As I'm writing this, I am still enrolled in university studies. I am expected to graduate in summer 2027.

If you we're intrested in my favorite NFL team, it always has been and always will be: #GoPackGo.
